Let’s take a look

Project overview

Short-term patient guidance model for the transition from hospital to home.

Problem

As part of their mission to assist patients in and out of hospital, the experts at CTI knew they needed to build an information portal with great SEO, security and smooth user experience. This is why they reached out to us at FABA.

Solution

We provide CTI with team members who are experienced with NextJS, NodeJS to tackle the project’s problems. We implement NextJS for SEO support. The website is set up on the platform for serverless deployment - Vercel - for greater scalability, more flexibility, and quicker time to release, all at a reduced cost. CTI had a requirement on Single Sign-On for a smooth user experience across multiple services so we made use of Auth0’s Single Sign-On feature.

The Process

Our process has been refined over our many successful projects, here’s how it worked with CTI. Upon receiving the design, we discussed the high-level business concepts and their expectations so we could suggest suitable services, then we drew a roadmap for the project. After FABA handled the implementation of the website, the result was delivered to our client via a staging environment, where they can review the UI, the structure, or the content before going to production. Our team brought high levels of technical acumen but that’s not the only thing needed for a successful project.

We understand the importance of frequent communication, therefore weekly meetings between FABA’s lead and CTI team are hosted to check up on the progress of the project. With transparent discussion, CTI can address any possible issues for review, and FABA can clarify any requirements for upcoming stages of the roadmap.

Features

  • CTI Publications: Visitors can access a number of healthcare-related articles, news and documents. Users with verified personal information can request accounts from CTI. Registered users can join Transition Coach’s group, access exclusive papers, or download Forms provided by CTI.
  • Coach Training: Applying for courses on understanding the CTI model, and practicing Transition coach skills and techniques.

Technologies

  • Javascript/Typescript
  • NextJS (based on ReactJS)
  • NodeJS
  • MongoDB for database
  • Vercel for deployments
  • Auth0
